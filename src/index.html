Get Advice

Give Advive

Good advice comes from people who already have experience in dealing with the topics that are being asked about. The internet is the perfect place to accumulate all of that experience and put it on a platform. Our goal is to build that platform where people with experience can come and help others seeking advice.

Anynomously

We are pursuing an anonymous platform because we have notice that people are more forthcoming about the advice they are looking for if their accounts are not associated with them personally. Sometimes people need advice on issues that can be very embarrassing. Staying anonymous allows for the person asking for advice to provide more context, which would allow them to get the help they need.
